# Mà (Conj 3)

### Situations

**S1:** Lan wanted to leave but forgot her raincoat. Her friend said:
<pre>
Lan lấy áo mưa của mình mà mặc.
(Here, Lan, you can use my raincoat.)
</pre>
**Mà** is used to give a friendly suggestion.

**S2:** Sơn's mother is blind, so he sells newspapers to make a living.
<pre>
Em Sơn phải bán báo mà nuôi mẹ.
(Sơn has to sell newspapers to feed his mother.)
</pre>
**Mà** indicates the purpose of selling newspapers (**nuôi mẹ**).

### Definition

**Mà**, as a conjunction, is used to give a suggestion, a request, or a command in a friendly way (**S1**) or to show the purpose of an action (**S2**).

**NOTES:** Do not use **mà** to give a request or make a suggestion to a person of higher position.

### Related Expressions

- **mà** and **để** / **để mà**:
**Mà** used in **S2** can be replaced with **để / để mà** to indicate purpose.
**Mà** is used in **S1** to make a suggestion or give a command (usually in short sentences). It can be combined with command words like **hãy**, **đi**.
<pre>
Xe đạp của tôi kìa! Hãy lấy xe tôi mà đi!
(My bicycle is over there. Take it and ride it!)
</pre>
